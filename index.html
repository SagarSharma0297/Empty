Quality Assurance Procedures

We undertake following steps in consumer behavior analysis studies to ensure quality of fieldwork. Besides scrutiny of the questionnaires, surprise visit and on-the-spot checks by the supervisor and other senior managers. More than 25% of all completed Questionnaires of each interviewer “back checked” by the supervisor with the Respondents, by re-contacting the same respondents and 5% of interviews back-checks by the Supervisors would again be back-checked by the Research.

LEARN MORE

Supervision

Each team will have an experienced Supervisor/Team leader to guide and assist the interviewers. He would also accompany them for the first few interviews. Thereafter he shall conduct random spot checks and back checks on the interviews conducted.

LEARN MORE

Training of Interviewers

To ensure quality and completeness of data, Training of Interviewers is vital. For all research project, we conduct a training program for 1-3 days depending on the objectives of the study, sensitivity of data, possible interviewers biases precautions needs, how to select respondents etc. Training also includes mock calls on the spot checks, back-checks and consistency checks on factual information’s.
